# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
rag-mcp-router community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

We expect everyone in the community to:

- Be kind, patient, and welcoming to others.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.

Behavior that makes others feel unwelcome or unsafe is not acceptable. If you
are unsure whether something is appropriate, assume good faith and err on the
side of courtesy.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues,
pull requests, discussions — and whenever an individual is representing the
project in public.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may
take appropriate, fair action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate,
including editing or removing contributions and, where warranted, restricting
participation.

Concerns may be reported to the maintainers at **noderguru@gmail.com**. All
reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion and respect for the
reporter's privacy.
